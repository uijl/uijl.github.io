About

My name is Joris den Uijl. I graduated as a hydraulic engineer from TU Delft in January ‘18. During my study I got more and more interested in “tech”. I started reading Hacker News, following ProductHunt and taught myself python programming. After my graduation I worked one year as a quantitave consultant in finance. Luckily I got a call from my former graduation supervisor and was able to join a large marine contractor as a Data Engineer.

I like data science/analytics/visualisation and python so most of my projects will comprise either analysing data or obtaining data. I’ll post all code as python notebooks on GitHub.

If you wish to contact feel free to send me a message on Twitter or connect with me on LinkedIn.
